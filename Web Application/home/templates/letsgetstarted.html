Note : For all the events except those which are organised during SHRUTHI and SUDHEE

Purpose of our website

To help club heads register their clubs and create or organize their club-events so as to enable students register easily into any event organized by any club

How can you get started?

If you are a club-head

step-1 : Use club signup to register your club

NOTE : During club-signup the username needs to be your clubname

step-2 : After signup wait for 24 hours for admin manual verification

step-3 : After 24 hours login into your account using the credentials you have given during the signup procedure

After Login

Step-4 : You can create an event

step-5 : The events you created will be stored in my events

You can either modify or delete those events

step-6 : If you want to change your password you can do that in My profile present at top right corner
